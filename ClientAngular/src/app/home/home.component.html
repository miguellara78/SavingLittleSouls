We proudly — Save Animals!

Hi, we are Saving Little Souls, a nonprofit organization with the sole purpose of rescuing abandoned, non wanted or orphaned domestic animals in order to find them a new loving long lasting home.

Real Homes

We are an allï¿½volunteer local organization. We do not utilize a shelter. All of our animals are fostered by volunteers in their loving homes until suitable and permanent families are found.

Non Profit

We rely 100% on your donations to support our efforts; to provide medical care, micro-chipping, to spay or neuter and save lives. All donations are tax deductible.

Volunteers

If you would like to join us in our efforts, we sure could use your help. Even just one day per month will make a world of difference. What ever works best for your schedule!

Rehabilitation

Our pets are evaluated, socialized, and given the loving care they need, prior to adoption. Pets have their basic shots and are spayed/neutered.
